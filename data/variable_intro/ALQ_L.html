National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey

August 2021-August 2023 Data Documentation, Codebook, and Frequencies

Alcohol Use (ALQ_L)

Data File: ALQ_L.xpt

First Published: September 2024
Last Revised: NA

Component Description

The alcohol use questionnaire (variable name prefix ALQ_L) file provides data on lifetime and current use (i.e., past 12 months, past 30 days). Questions are not specific to the type of alcohol used.

Eligible Sample

All participants ages 12 years and older in the August 2021-August 2023 sample were eligible; however, only data from participants who were ages 18 and over are included in this file.

A special use data file for participants ages 12-17, ALQY_L_R, is available through the NCHS Research Data Center (RDC).

Interview Setting and Mode of Administration

The ALQ section was administered at the Mobile Examination Center (MEC) during the MEC Interview, including a question on binge drinking frequency in the past 30 days (i.e., ALQ170), which was previously asked at the end of the in-person dietary interview in the MEC. For adults 18 years and older, questions were self-administered using the Audio-Computer Assisted Self-Interview (ACASI) system. The ACASI enables participants to hear questions through headphones as well as read questions on the computer screen. Participants move at their own speed and touch the screen to indicate their response. For adolescents 12 to 17 whose data are available via the NCHS RDC, questions were also self-administered using the ACASI system. 

The NHANES August 2021-August 2023 ALQ is available on the NHANES website. 

Quality Assurance & Quality Control

The ACASI system is programmed with built-in consistency checks to reduce data entry errors. ACASI also uses online help screens to assist participants in defining key terms used in the questionnaire.

For details on the QA/QC process for this component, please refer to the August 2021-August 2023 MEC ACASI Procedures Manual on the NHANES website.

Data Processing and Editing

The data were edited for completeness, consistency, and illogical values. Because the data are based on self-reports, not all inconsistencies could be resolved. It is recommended that analysts review the data carefully prior to analysis.

Analytic Notes

The NHANES alcohol use questions for adults were not changed between the 2017-March 2020 and August 2021-August 2023 data collection cycles. However, question ALQ290 (i.e., “During the past 12 months, about how often did {you/SP} have 12 or more drinks in a single day?”) was removed from the August 2021-August 2023 questionnaire.

The NHANES August 2021-August 2023 alcohol use data were collected in the MEC; therefore, exam sample weights should be used for the analyses. Please refer to the NHANES Analytic Guidelines and the on-line NHANES Tutorial for details on the use of sample weights and other analytic issues.
